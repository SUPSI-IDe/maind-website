How to apply

Tuition fees
15'000.– CHF (MAS)
6’000.– CHF (CAS)
Scholarships
Partial scholarships are available upon the evaluation of portfolios and CVs. Winners are entitled to a reduction worth up to 3’000.– CHF each.
Application deadline
31 March 2017
Courses start
18 September 2017
  1. Application form
  2. Interview
  3. Enrolment

Application form

Candidates must fill in and send the application form with the required documentation attached duly completed (find below important deadlines and the application form).

Interview

Selected applicants will be invited for an interview (over Skype or on the phone) with a representative of the board of the MAS program.

Enrolment

Successful applicants will receive the confirmation of registration to the MAS program and the notification of award of the partial scholarship. The payment of the first instalment, within the proper deadline, officially completes the registration of the candidate to the MAS program.
